# AI Guidance & Skills

This folder contains AI guidance files, skills, and instructions to customize your preferred AI environment.

Use these resources to:
- **Configure your AI assistant** with domain-specific knowledge and best practices
- **Define custom skills** for specialized workflows (testing, refactoring, architecture, etc.)
- **Tailor prompts and instructions** to match your development style and project needs
- **Share AI configurations** across your team and organization

Each file in this folder can be integrated into your AI tool's customization settings to enhance code generation, debugging, and architectural guidance.
